Getting Started

The platform is split into two distinct portals to ensure security and streamlined workflows.

Admin Login

Accessed via the secure admin directory. Requires administrative credentials. The login page features a professional layout synchronized with the school's branding.

Student Login

Students login using their unique Roll Number or ID. This ensures that every attempt is tied to a specific academic record.

The Administrative Powerhouse

1. The Admin Dashboard

Upon logging in, administrators are greeted with a high-level overview of the school's activity:

  • Live Stats: View total subjects, exams, students, and feedback at a glance.
  • Recent Students: A quick list of the latest registered students.
  • Recent Submissions: Real-time tracking of students finishing their exams, showing their names and instant scores.

2. Subject & Exam Management

Manage Courses

Create and organize the school curriculum. Add subjects like Mathematics, English, or Sciences to the global subject bank.

Class-Based Exams

Exams are organized by class (JSS3, SS1, etc.). Admin can add new exams, set time limits, and define how many questions should be displayed.

Question Bank

Add Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) for each exam. Specify the options and the correct answer for automated grading.

3. Student Registration

Manage the entire student body with ease. Students are grouped by their class levels, making it simple to track results by academic year.

The Student Experience

1. Personalized Home

When a student logs in, they see a dashboard tailored to their class:

  • Available Exams: A list of all exams currently open for their class.
  • Subjects: A summary of the subjects they are enrolled in.
  • Status Tracking: See how many exams they have completed and their current position.

2. The CBT Environment

The examination interface is designed for focus and speed:

  • Timer: A live countdown timer keeps students aware of the remaining time.
  • Navigation: Easy-to-use interface to cycle through questions.
  • Auto-Submit: Submissions are processed immediately upon completion, ensuring data integrity.

Results & Performance Tracking

Instant Grading

As soon as a student clicks "Submit", the system calculates their score based on the correct answers stored in the database.

Exam Attendance

Admin can view exactly who took which exam, what time they finished, and their final percentage score.

Comparative Rankings

The system ranks students by their performance, helping teachers identify top performers and students who need extra support.
